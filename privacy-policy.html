Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 2026

At HimYog, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring that your personal information is handled in a safe and responsible manner.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information that you provide directly to us when you fill out an enquiry form, make a booking, or communicate with us via WhatsApp or email. This may include:

  • Full name and contact details (email, phone number).
  • Yoga background and health goals.
  • Payment information (processed securely through third-party gateways).
  • Travel details for airport transfers.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Process your course bookings and reservations.
  • Respond to your enquiries and provide customer support.
  • Send important updates regarding your course or retreat.
  • Improve our services and website experience.

3. Data Protection

We implement a variety of security measures to maintain the safety of your personal information. Your data is never sold, traded, or otherwise transferred to outside parties, except for trusted third parties who assist us in operating our website and conducting our business (such as payment gateways).

4. Your Rights

You have the right to request a copy of the information we hold about you or ask us to delete your personal data from our records at any time.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding this Privacy Policy, you may contact us at weareonetechnation@gmail.com.
